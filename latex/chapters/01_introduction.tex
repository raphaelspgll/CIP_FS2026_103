\section{Introduction}

The cryptocurrency market is characterised by high volatility and continuous operation, making it an attractive domain for short-term predictive modelling.
The central research question of this project is: \emph{can publicly available market indicators from day $t-1$ predict the direction of price movement on day $t$?}

Three research questions guide the work:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em,itemsep=0pt]
  \item Which market indicators have the strongest relationship with daily cryptocurrency price changes?
  \item How accurately can historical indicators from day $t-1$ predict price direction on day $t$?
  \item Which machine learning model achieves the highest accuracy under these conditions?
\end{enumerate}

\noindent The pipeline covers six coins: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Binance Coin (BNB), Solana (SOL), Ripple (XRP), and Internet Computer (ICP).
