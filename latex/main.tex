\vspace{1.5cm}
\noindent\textbf{Abstract.}
This report presents a binary classification pipeline to predict the daily price movement direction (up / down) of six major cryptocurrencies using publicly available market indicators from the preceding day.
Historical data spanning three years was collected from CoinGecko via a Scrapy--Playwright spider, cleaned, and enriched with technical features (moving averages, volatility, volume change).
Three classifiers were evaluated under a strict chronological train/test split: Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, and Linear Regression used as a classifier.
Results and design decisions are discussed, with particular attention to preventing look-ahead bias in time-series settings.
